What it does

Watches your folders

Monitors Downloads, Pictures, and Screenshots for new files as they arrive.

Gives you time

Set a 5, 10, 15, or 30 minute delay. Use your files first, they get swept after.

Cancel any time

See a file you want to keep? Hit the cancel button before it sweeps.

Undo sweeps

Changed your mind? One click puts the file right back where it was.

Image previews

Thumbnails for images in the queue so you know exactly what's about to be swept.

Tracks your savings

See how much space you've freed up, persisted across sessions.

How it works
1
Run the command. A dashboard opens in your browser at localhost.
2
Pick your delay. Choose how long files sit before being swept.
3
Hit Start. New downloads and screenshots appear in the queue with a countdown.
4
Files get organized. Images, documents, videos, audio, and archives each go to their own folder in ~/FileVault/collected/.
